SONIC THE HEDGEHOG

THE HERO

The fastest thing alive, and he knows it. Sonic is brash, confident, and always has a quip ready — but beneath the attitude is a genuine hero who will never abandon a friend. In Sonic Velocity Comics, we explore a slightly older Sonic grappling with what it means to be a symbol of hope in a complicated world.

His rivalry with Shadow, his bond with Tails, and his complicated feelings about fame all come to a head during the Chaos Protocol arc.

MILES "TAILS" PROWER

THE INVENTOR

Tails is the brains behind most of Team Sonic's victories — even if Sonic won't always admit it. With an IQ that rivals Eggman's and a heart twice as big, Tails is the emotional core of Sonic Velocity Comics. Issue #03 is entirely his story, and it's one of our most beloved.

His twin tails allow him to fly like a helicopter, and his workshop houses gadgets that would make any engineer weep with envy.

KNUCKLES THE ECHIDNA

THE GUARDIAN

Last of the Knuckle Clan, guardian of the Master Emerald on Angel Island. Knuckles is stubborn, serious, and occasionally very gullible — but in a fight, there's nobody you'd rather have in your corner. His fists can shatter boulders, and his loyalty is unbreakable.

Issue #04 places Knuckles in the spotlight during the Emerald Tide storyline, testing his skills against Eggman's most ruthless Badniks.

AMY ROSE

THE WARRIOR

Don't let the pink fool you. Amy Rose wields her Piko Piko Hammer with devastating precision, and her determination could outlast a Chaos Emerald. In Sonic Velocity Comics, Amy has grown from a lovestruck fan into a true warrior with her own goals and her own story to tell.

Special Issue #1 is dedicated entirely to her — and fans have called it one of the best single issues we've produced.

SHADOW THE HEDGEHOG

THE RIVAL

The Ultimate Life Form. Created by Gerald Robotnik, Shadow exists at the intersection of science and tragedy. He matches Sonic in speed and surpasses him in raw Chaos power — and unlike Sonic, he operates without a rulebook. Ally? Enemy? It depends on the day.

Shadow is the driving force behind issue #05 — the most intense issue we've written. His confrontation with Sonic is months in the making.

DR. EGGMAN

THE VILLAIN

Doctor Ivo "Eggman" Robotnik. IQ of 300. Owner of the Eggman Empire. Collector of defeats at the hands of a blue hedgehog. Eggman is simultaneously the most dangerous scientist on the planet and a figure of genuine comedic tragedy — always one scheme away from world domination, never quite getting there.

In Sonic Velocity Comics, we give Eggman more depth than ever: his obsession isn't mere villainy. It's a mission. And that makes him truly terrifying.

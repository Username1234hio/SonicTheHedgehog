ABOUT US

A love letter to a blue hedgehog and 30+ years of history.

THE PROJECT

Sonic Velocity Comics is a passion project born from a deep love of the Sonic the Hedgehog franchise. What started as a few sketches in a notebook has grown into a full fan-made comic series with thousands of readers around the world.

We are not affiliated with SEGA or any official Sonic property. This is created entirely for fun, distributed completely free, and made with respect for the original creators and the incredible legacy they built.

Every issue is written, illustrated, and lettered by a small team of dedicated fans working in their spare time. We aim to release one issue per month, with special standalone issues scattered throughout the year.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is this official? Are you affiliated with SEGA?
No — this is entirely fan-made and non-commercial. We have no affiliation with SEGA, Sonic Team, or any official Sonic licensees. All Sonic characters belong to SEGA.
How often do new issues release?
We aim for one main issue per month, typically at the end of the month. Special standalone issues are released occasionally throughout the year. Follow us on social media for release announcements.
Is everything really free?
Yes! Every issue is completely free to read online and download. We will never charge for our fan comics. You can support us by sharing the site with other Sonic fans.
Can I submit fan art or guest content?
Absolutely! We love featuring fan art from the community. Send your submissions to our email address (on this page) with the subject line "FAN ART SUBMISSION." We'll credit you fully and feature it on our social channels.
Can I join the team?
We occasionally open spots for artists and writers. When we're looking, we'll post about it on social media. Keep an eye out — and in the meantime, build your portfolio!
Where can I read the comics?
Right here on this site! Head to the Issues page to read online or download each issue as a PDF. No account required.
